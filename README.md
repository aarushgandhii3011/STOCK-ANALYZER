# STOCK-ANALYZER
📈 How It Works The script processes historical stock data and applies the indicators. A Buy Signal is typically generated when: Price is above the Supertrend (Uptrend). Price is trading above the VWAP. The ADX is greater than 25, confirming the trend isn't just "noise."

## 🛠️ THE TECH STACK
- Python 3.x 🐍 (The Brains)
Pandas 🐼 (Data Sorcery)
NumPy 🔢 (Lightning Fast Math)


### 💎 INDICATOR SHOWCASE
- 1. ⚖️ VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)
The Institutional Anchor. It’s the "fair value" for the day. 🏛️
The Vibe: It’s the price level that big banks and hedge funds care about most. 🏦
Bullish: Price Above VWAP 📈 🟢
Bearish: Price Below VWAP 📉 🔴
Utility: Perfect for catching intraday entries and exits. 🎯




- 2. ⚡ ADX (Average Directional Index)
The Trend Power Meter. It tells you if the move is "Real" or just "Noise." 🔊
The Vibe: Don't get caught in a "dead" market. 😴
ADX > 25: TRENDING HARD. 🚀 (Time to make money!)
ADX < 20: SIDEWAYS CHOP. 🦀 (Keep your hands in your pockets!)
Utility: The ultimate filter to avoid "fake-outs." 🛡️



- 3. 🛡️ SUPERTREND
The Trend Bodyguard. A dynamic, volatility-based trailing stop-loss. 🤺
The Vibe: Stay in the winner until the very end. 🏁
Green Signal: BUY / HOLD 🟢✨
Red Signal: SELL / SHORT 🔴🔥
Utility: Automatically follows the price, protecting your profits while you sleep. 💤💰
